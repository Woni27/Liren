About Us

Comprehensive apparel enterprise integrating design, production, processing, trade, and sales.

Founded in 2012 in the coastal city of Dalian, China, we focus on premium women’s outerwear—including artificial fur (eco‑friendly), fleece jackets, shearling coats, and down jackets. Since 2021, we have been a trusted OEM supplier for South Korea’s Samyang Group.

Our mission is to deliver sustainable, fashion‑forward products backed by rigorous quality control, timely delivery, and transparent communication.

  • Multilingual trade team: Korean · English · Chinese
  • Flexible MOQs—small runs to large programs
  • End‑to‑end support: sampling → bulk → logistics
  • Stable, audited partner factories
